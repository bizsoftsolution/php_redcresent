Red Crescent Vision & Mission

 

 

  1. Our vision
    Human dignity and peace throughout the world

    Our mission
    Prevent and alleviate human suffering through humanitarian activities and actions

    Our strategic aim
    1. 1)Save lives, protect livelihoods, and strengthen recovery from disasters and crises
  2. 2)Enable healthy and safe living
  3. 3)Promote social inclusion and a culture of non-violence and peace

Our motto
“Saving lives, changing minds”
